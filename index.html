About Us

St. Louis Lawn Care has been serving the community for over a decade, providing top-quality lawn maintenance, landscaping, and garden care services. Our mission is to enhance the beauty of St. Louis, one lawn at a time. We take pride in our commitment to excellence, attention to detail, and eco-friendly lawn care solutions.

Our Services

We offer a full range of lawn care services to keep your yard looking its best:

  • Lawn Mowing: Regular mowing to keep your grass at an optimal height for healthy growth.
  • Fertilization & Weed Control: Eco-friendly treatments to promote lush grass while eliminating weeds.
  • Aeration & Seeding: Improve soil health and promote strong root growth with our aeration and overseeding services.
  • Seasonal Cleanups: We handle leaf removal, debris clearing, and garden maintenance to prepare your yard for any season.
